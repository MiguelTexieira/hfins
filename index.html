The Process

From Street to Sea

How old decks become ocean-ready fins in four steps

1

Source & Select

I collect worn skateboards from local skate shops, parks, and fellow riders. Each deck is inspected for structural integrity and that perfect graphic that deserves to keep living.

2

Template & Trace

Using proven fin templates, I carefully plan each cut to maximize the deck's graphics and grain patterns. The maple layers create natural color variations that make each fin unique.

3

Shape & Foil

Each fin is hand-shaped and foiled for optimal water flow. The laminated maple layers are sanded to reveal stunning cross-sections of color â€” every pass of the sander unveils new patterns.

4

Seal & Finish

Multiple coats of marine-grade epoxy seal the fins for ocean durability. The final product is glassed, polished, and ready to rip â€” carrying the spirit of the skatepark into the lineup.
